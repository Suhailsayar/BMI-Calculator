⚖️ BMI CalculatorA simple, sleek, and accurate way to check your Body Mass Index.
🔗 Live Link: [bmi-calculator-git-main-suhail-s-projects-398aeda5.vercel.app]
🌟 About the ProjectI built this BMI Calculator to provide a quick,
no-nonsense tool for anyone looking to monitor their health metrics
. It takes the guesswork out of the math and gives you an instant categorization of
your weight status based on global health standards.
✨ FeaturesReal-time Results: No page refreshes—get your score instantly.
Health Status Feedback: Clear indicators for Underweight, Normal, Overweight, and Obese categories.
Mobile First: Designed to look great on your phone, tablet, or desktop.
Error Handling: Prevents unrealistic inputs to ensure accuracy.
🧮 The Math Behind It:This app uses the standard metric formula to calculate your Body Mass Index:
BMI = weight/Height*Height
🛠️ Tech Stack
HTML5 - Semantic structure.
CSS3 - Custom styling with a focus on UX.
JavaScript (ES6+) - Logic and DOM manipulation.
